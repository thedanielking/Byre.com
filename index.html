How it works

streamlining your buying/hiring processes

01

Sign Up

Start by signing up for our SaaS platform and scrolling through several business thumbnails and profile pages. Filter your search to fit what you are looking for in particular.

02

Buy/Hire

Buy the products or hire the service you need by contacting the business owners through clicking the "Buy" or "Hire" button. Negotitate(if applicable) with the sellers in our in-chat app system.

03

Payment

If satisfied with the products or services and negotiation price(if applicable), make a move by paying into our escrow system and it will get delivered when you are satisfied with the product or service.

04

Access Time

Experience the flexibility of accessing our SaaS platform anytime, anywhere, on any device, enabling location-based search from where you are.

Faq's

Sign up as a seller by clicking the "Sign Up" button and choosing the "seller" option. Upload your business profile photo, images and videos of your product or service on your store.

During the hiring and buying process, you pay into an escrow account with us. The money is only transferred if you are satisfied with your product or service. You also get to read reviews and ratings of each business owner before purchase.

We do not disclose the location of online business owners. For those businesses that are on-site, we only disclose the information about your location at your approval.

Byre gives each business a dashboard overview that gives analysis on your business sales, bookings, revenue, etc. Byre keeps your business accountable to you!
